About Me
Hi, I am a 3rd-year Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Kai Chen. Before that, I received my B.E. degree in Electronic Science and Technology from Zhejiang University in 2021. My research interests include datacenter networking, in-network acceleration, and machine learning systems. If you are interested in my works, feel free to drop me an email.
