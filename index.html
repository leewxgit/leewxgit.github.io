About Me

Hi, I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Kai Chen. Before that, I received my B.E. degree in Electronic Science and Technology from Zhejiang University in 2021. I am broadly interested in datacenter networking and distributed systems, with particular focuses on network protocols, high-speed networking, and networked system for AI. If you are interested in my works, feel free to drop me an email.
